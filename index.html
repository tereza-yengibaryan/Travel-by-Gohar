Why Visit Armenia

Armenia is one of the oldest countries in the world with a history dating back thousands of years.

Armenia became the first country to adopt Christianity as a state religion in 301 AD, creating a rich spiritual heritage.

From Lake Sevan and Dilijan forests to mountain landscapes, deep canyons, and ancient monasteries, Armenia offers incredible diversity for travelers.

Visitors are welcomed not as tourists, but as guests, experiencing genuine Armenian hospitality and unforgettable memories.

Armenia is also famous for ancient winemaking traditions and authentic cuisine made with fresh mountain ingredients.
